Welcome to Artisan Candles

We create beautiful, handcrafted candles using only the finest natural ingredients. Each candle is carefully made with soy wax, pure essential oils, and lead-free wicks.

Our passion for candle making began over a decade ago, and we continue to uphold the tradition of quality craftsmanship while embracing sustainable practices.

100% Natural Wax

Our candles are made from pure soy wax, which burns cleaner and longer than paraffin.

Essential Oil Scents

We use only pure essential oils for fragrance, never synthetic perfumes or chemicals.

Eco-Friendly Packaging

All our packaging is recyclable or biodegradable, supporting our commitment to sustainability.

Our Candle Making Process

Each candle is carefully hand-poured in small batches to ensure quality and consistency. We monitor temperature precisely and cure each candle for optimal scent throw and burn performance.

From selecting the perfect wax blend to testing each fragrance combination, our process is built on attention to detail and passion for the craft.

Why Choose Soy Wax?

Soy wax is a natural, renewable resource that burns cleaner than paraffin wax. It doesn't release harmful toxins into the air and is biodegradable.

Our soy wax candles have a longer burn time, better scent throw, and easier cleanup with just soap and water.

  • Cleaner burning than paraffin
  • Longer lasting
  • Biodegradable and renewable
  • Supports local farmers
